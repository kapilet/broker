PAS Integration

Brokerstar works independently, but best if it is integrated with an existing Policy Administration System. We will discuss with you the capability to integrate:

  • Inbound Policy information mapped from risk capture and rate calculation
  • Automated synchronisation of tasks/diaries
  • Automated synchronisation of key policy activities for reflection in customer portal
  • Document access outbound from PAS
  • Document upload inbound to PAS
  • Financial information for collation into MI and Target comparison
