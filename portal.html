Customer Portal

Deliver 24/7 expert guidance to your clients

Having a dynamic customer portal can make a huge difference if it is possible for customers to self-serve and also feel they are getting a better response from a broker via this method than by calling and speaking to staff directly

Benefits

Save Time with Online Servicing

Put more time back in your day by offering your clients a 24/7, online portal. By allowing your clients to self-serve, they can get the information they need around the clock. Dramatically reduce the amount of time spent servicing and marketing to your clients.

Quickly Provide Value

Get your clients up and running in no time. With the client portal, user profiles are generated automatically and come prepopulated with pages, colour and content. Clients are notified automatically through the system once it's ready for them to sign in.

Boost Client Engagement

Stay in front of clients without adding extra work, with automatic content postings and emails branded back to your business. Increase retention and reduce the amount of time spent servicing clients, so you can focus on prospecting and growing your brokerage instead.
