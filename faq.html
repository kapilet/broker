FAQ's

Brokerstar is a range of configurable component modules to enable you to digitise your customer journey at a pace that suits you. Prioritise the components that provide the greatest payback to your business and add more functionality as you continue to digitise your business.

We map all lead providers and all classes into our lead module. All website leads can use configurable webservice or email import mappings. brokerstar comes with fully configurable internal and public questionsets which automatically populate any number of these as leads. Public questionsets can be hosted on your own websites to collect leads and risk details automatically populated in brokerstar and quoted online if required

Brokerstar provides tools for bulk data upload. It will provide you with templates which you can fill in using Excel and bulk upload your data. brokerstar will pre-validate the upload to ensure the data is correct. The data won't flow into your process until it is checked and released by your team.

Brokerstar comes with Marketing Automation as a fully integrated module allowing you to set up multiple marketing and response campaigns to customers. These include sending marketing messages via e-mail/sms/whatsap as well as measuring their response in opening/clicking links and filling in public questionsets. You can configure questionsets within brokerstar and automatically collect responses which are recorded against the prospect/customer

All inbound communications can be connected into brokerstar so that they appear in leads and policies automatically - whenever a customer or prospect is communicated to or communicates in, this is captured against their details. This includes e-mail inboxes, SMS and whatsapp messages as well as Customer Portal communications. These can be managed with rule-based processes within brokerstar including distribution and automated processes including keyword identification, replies, tasks questionset generation and link back to customers to take the next step. All outbound communications by e-mail/sms/whatsapp/customer portal can be handled through brokerstar depending on contact preferences. We will work with you to route required emails, SMS and WhatsApp in and out as required

All leads are tracked by full lifecycle date/time and status within brokerstar so it is possible to see all of the steps in their processing and analyse every step of the sales process, including conversion. These can be tweaked to improve each step and overall conversion. pre-configured KPIs and reports track conversion ratios using a range of criteria

Brokerstar comes with fully configurable questionsets and templates which we will work with you to capture risk information most efficiently. These complete with a rate calculator where a panel of quotes are derived by a combination of insurer portal automation, insurer API, rate calculation scripts or manual input

All questionsets can be copied so that the complete risk information set is separately created and can then be independently amended and quoted

After one year an existing risk quote, whether on cover or prospect can be copied for renewal with rules such as NCB increment and re-validated with customer/prospect to give a next year quote

Brokerstar facilitates a range of communication methods. You can create or upload a document in brokerstar and send it out or auto-upload to the customer portal as well as remove from the portal

We will look to integrate your add-on provider through portal automation or API to process add-on purchases automatically and generate bordereaus automatically. We will market your add-ons to customers as their landing page in the customer portal. brokerstar supports marketing of add-ons and online purchase

We will work with you to determine the required integration points and if your policy administration system supports these we will implement the required touchpoints and integration

We will work with you to create a range of MTA questionsets which the customer can fill in via email link or customer portal. The MTA requirements can then be populated and these appear in brokerstar automatically and can be processed and the completion response returned to the customer as well as a payment link from your payment provider

Each outstanding requirement can be created from a batch depending on class of business and other specific factors. These requirements appear on the customer portal automatically and the customer requested to upload documents against them. Each upload is then processed within brokerstar for approval or re-upload and the status reflecting in both brokerstar and customer portal

All requirements and outstanding payments (if integrated) appear against the customer and will automatically be chased by brokerstar. We work with you to set up a range of rules for automated chasing or manual override for all of this information which brokerstar will then handle via the customer's preferred method of contact. Any unfulfilled chases will appear in exception lists for manual handling at critical points/times in the process

We will look at each task type and integrate a required process into our communication and customer portal modules as well as our admin module for processing and if possible will look to automate that process through portal automation or API with the provider such as the insurer's MID portal

This depends on what output is required. For standardised processes many aspects are plug and play. For niche ratings, we need to work with you to determine your unique questionsets and detailed mappings to generate quotation rates from various providers through your chosen method

Typically a scoping exercise takes about a month if it is agreed in advance that necessary time is allocated by both you and us. Implementation can take about a month to get unified configured questionsets followed by several weeks per quote integration. Other modules typically take around three months to adopt and integrate processes

Brokerstar has been deliberately designed to give a high degree of configurable flexibility for all the module components. This can be done by us initially and handed over to your team over time to better reflect your specific business processes and preferences. These changes do not require a separate supported branch of the software, it is all implicit configuration, not bespoke development

We have an implementation manager from our side and request one from your side as well. Group calls with these two managers and other resources as required will happen from once a week to once a day if required during the implementation phase. These calls are on teams and usually recorded to help teams to review details of discussions off-line

As part of our agreement we will hand hold configuration up to a certain amount of dedicated team role time for the first 6 months of implementation. After that time we will provide technical support through video/screen-sharing and email as well as professional services if required

The approach we take is to implement in a schedule one component module at a time. As this happens your business sees a return on investment in terms of increased turnover and greater efficiencies and justifies future modules. The exact price depends on the combination of modules and professional services dedicated to the project
