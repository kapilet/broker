Marketing Automation

The days of pushing one-off emails to clients and customers are over; there is too great a margin of error and time spent on customisation. E-mail marketing automation systems help you quickly push out communications to prospects and clients and also help you track results. Having an integrated marketing component in brokerstar ensures a continuous loop in communication without specialist integration capabilities

Being able to follow up with customer prospects, or even existing customers for specific purposes, and follow the interactivity of the customer to respond to the campaign is critical for the engagement and warming of prospects

Sending a link to a custom questionset means that the response of customer prospects to a particular campaign can be automatically tracked and picked up within the brokerstar administration system by staff and followed up, either with subsequent campaign steps or by staff stepping through brokerstar processes directly

All interactions linking e-mail and whatsapp campaigns to data-capture processes online are custom configured, there is no software development process and if required can all be built in-house

Marketing Automation offers these key features

  • Multiple campaigns build on the internal brokerstar lead set
  • Each campaign having the capacity for multiple steps based on customer response
  • Mail delivery/Read/Open/Individual link click
  • Unique Link to data-capture online with subsequent actions based on captured response
