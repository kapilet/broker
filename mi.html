MI Analysis

If all information is being captured by brokerstar, then the entire business can be monitored through reports and KPIs. These can be on a per-role basis against target and team performance, or on a management level to ensure the business is tracking and taking intelligent action in a timely manner

The reports and KPIs provided by brokerstar are based on best of practice analysis, but they are not limited to these. Self-defined KPIs and specific analysis and targets can be self-configured into brokerstar

Examples of analysis are:

  • Numbers and sources of leads vs outcomes
  • Staff performance
  • Imported claims data vs income for loss-ratio approximation
  • Conversion ratios from application to quote/quote to re-quote/NTU/On-Cover
  • Class vs income levels and targets
  • Inbound lead volume
  • Inbound lead quality
  • Time spent per lead
  • Time spent per policy
  • Time spent per Â£ income
